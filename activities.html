Recent Reading Groups

Topological Data Analysis (TDA) | Spring 2025

  • Goal: Introduction to the subject of Topological Data Analysis. We explain the mathematical foundations of TDA (such as persistent homology) as well as its integration into machine learning.

  • Where?: LaMME, Université d’Évry.

  • When?: May-June 2025.

  • Program (R. Martos and V. Runge).

\(\infty\)-catégories | Winter 2023/2024

  • Goal: Introduction to \(\infty\)-categories (avoiding model categories).

  • Where?: LPP, Université de Lille.

  • When?: December-February 2023/2024.

  • Program (A. Touzé).

Non-local games and quantum groups | Winter 2019

  • Goal: Study the connection between non-local games and compact quantum groups in the context of quantum information theory after the work of Laura Mančiska and D. E. Roberson (among others).

  • Where?: Department of Mathematical Sciences and the QMATH Centre, University of Copenhagen.

  • When?: January-March 2019.

  • Program (R. Martos).
